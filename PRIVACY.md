# Privacy Policy for CamPilot

**Last Updated**: January 18, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. **CamPilot** is designed to prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app interacts with your data.

### 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**
CamPilot operates entirely locally on your devices using Peer-to-Peer (P2P) technology. We do not have servers, and we do not track your usage.

### 2. Permissions & Usage
To function correctly, CamPilot requires the following permissions:

*   **Camera**: Required to capture photos and videos.
*   **Local Network**: Required to discover and connect to your other devices nearby.
*   **Photo Library**: Required to save captured photos and videos to your device's Camera Roll.
*   **Microphone**: Required to record audio when capturing videos.

All data captured (photos/videos) is stored directly on your local device. It is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at via our [Support Page](SUPPORT.md).
