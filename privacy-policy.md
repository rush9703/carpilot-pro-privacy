# Privacy Policy for CarPilot

**Last Updated: December 24, 2025**

## Introduction

CarPilot ("we", "our", or "the App") is a vehicle performance testing and driving data recording application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Data We Collect

The App collects the following data **only while you are actively recording**:

- **Location Data (GPS)**: Used to track your driving route, speed, and position
- **Motion Data**: Accelerometer and gyroscope data for vehicle dynamics analysis
- **Audio Levels**: Ambient noise measurement (sound levels only, no voice recording)
- **Weather Information**: Fetched from Apple WeatherKit based on your location

## How Data is Stored

- **All data is stored locally on your device only**
- We do **NOT** upload any data to external servers
- We do **NOT** share your data with any third parties
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time within the App

## Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to:
- Display real-time driving metrics during recording
- Generate performance analysis reports
- Provide lap timing and track analysis features

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete any recorded session at any time
- Uninstall the App to remove all data

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple WeatherKit for weather information. Weather requests are made based on your approximate location. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
