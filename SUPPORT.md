# CamPilot Support

Welcome to the support page for **CamPilot**.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I connect two devices?**
A: Ensure both devices have Wi-Fi and Bluetooth enabled. Open CamPilot on both devices. Select "Camera" on one device and "Remote" on the other. They should discover each other automatically. Tap the device name to connect.

**Q: Why can't I find the other device?**
A:
1. Make sure "Local Network" permission is granted in iOS Settings -> CamPilot.
2. Check if both devices are close to each other.
3. Try toggling Wi-Fi off and on again.

**Q: Where are my photos saved?**
A: Photos and videos are saved directly to the **Photos app** (system gallery) on the device acting as the **Camera**.
